# Contributing to the Forgotten Runes Community Compendium

This document describes **how** to submit, **how** submissions are evaluated, and **how** decisions are made.

---

## Submission Requirements

### Context
- Must be set in the **Runiverse**
- Must feature **one clearly identifiable primary FRWC token**

### Framing (Optional)
Canon events may be referenced but are not required:
- The Burning
- The Opening of the Seventh Gate
- The Arrival of the Warriors

### Narrative Aim
Not lore exposition.

Stories should evoke **wonder, discovery, and tension** in an unexplored universe.

### Length
- **6,000–10,000 words** per story
- Target compendium length: ~60,000 words total

### Style Cohesion
Submission implies agreement to **substantive editorial rewrites** for tone, pacing, structure, and cohesion across the book.

## Selection & Governance

- The project lead may:
  - appoint a panel of up to five judges
  - remove non-performing judges
  - veto inclusion or exclusion of any submission
- No obligation exists to publish any submission.

Judges provide advisory input only.

---

## Editorial Rubric

### 1. Core Eligibility (Hard Gates)
Failure on any item → **automatic rejection**

- Set recognisably in the Runiverse  
- One identifiable primary FRWC token  
- Word count 6,000–10,000  
- Complete narrative  
- Agreement to substantive editing  
- No plagiarism or undisclosed AI use  

---

### 2. Narrative Quality (40%)

- Narrative coherence  
- Tension & stakes  
- Resolution integrity  

*Bias: clean execution over experimental ambition.*

---

### 3. World Integration (25%)

- Token centrality  
- Canon adjacency  
- Runiverse texture  

---

### 4. Conceptual Signal (20%)

- Core speculative idea  
- Originality within constraints  
- Lingering impact (“aftertaste”)

---

### 5. Style & Editability (15%)

- Prose clarity  
- Voice malleability  
- Structural fixability  

---

## Decisions

- **Accept** — strong; limited revision  
- **Conditional Accept** — strong core; targeted rewrite required  
- **Reject** — fails eligibility or requires reconceptualisation  

Scores inform discussion but do **not** bind final decisions.

---

## Extension & Kill Criteria (Full)

- If ≥2 stories received in first 30 days → extend submissions by 30 days  
- Hard submission cap: 60 days total  

The project terminates if:
1. <2 stories in first 30 days  
2. <7 stories by end of extension  
3. First 4 submissions are rejected
